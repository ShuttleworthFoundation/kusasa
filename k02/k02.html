Kusasa Level 2

While intended for grade five learners, the eight modules that make up Kusasa Level 2 are suitable for grade 6 learners who have not yet been exposed to eToys. These modules are probably not beyond the capacity of some grade four learners too.
Kusasa level 2

Kusasa levels 1, 2 and 3 are packaged together in LAMS modules which can be downloaded here. The stories are contained within the LAMS modules, however all the stories are also accessible online. You can read the Kusasa Level 2 stories now in your browser using the links below, or you can download the stories here. The eToys projects are not contained within the LAMS modules.
