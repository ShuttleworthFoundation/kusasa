New ways of thinking

Kusasa is designed to introduce learners and teachers to new ways of thinking in mathematics, science and other learning areas. Through Kusasa teachers and learners will explore and practise:
• analytical thinking;
• creative thinking;
• scientific thinking;
• systems thinking;
• dynamic thinking;
• consequential thinking;
• contextual thinking;
• conceptual thinking;
• incremental thinking;
• thinking in questions;
• thinking out of the box;
• thinking in essentials;
• thinking in principles;
• thinking errors;
• and much more.

Each of these kinds of thinking will be explored in the context of a comic, through a classroom activity, and in an eToys programming challenge. In this way learners will experience these different kinds of thinking in different contexts, making for a richer and deeper learning experience.
