Changes

Imagine you're living in 1907. You're visited by a time traveller who offers you a chance to take a journey through time. You choose to visit the world of 2007, because you want to discover how much the world can change in just 100 years. Arriving in 2007 you are shocked at the spectacular progress in technology. Automotive, aeronautical, electrical and electronic engineering have produced technologies unimaginable 100 years ago. But there is one place you find instantly recognisable: school. It would seem that the more things change, the more school stays the same!

Kusasa recognises the opportunity to close the gap between the changes taking place out in the real world, and the resistance to change so common among so many schools. It's about time!
