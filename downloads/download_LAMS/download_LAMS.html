Kusasa LAMS modules

Each Kusasa LAMS module usually includes:
  • a story;
  • questions to help learners reflect on or "crunch" the ideas in the story;
  • a physical classroom activity to prepare for eToys;
  • questions to help learners "crunch" the activity;
  • questions to help learners "crunch" the eToys project.

Please notice that the eToys projects are not packaged within the LAMS modules. You will need to download the eToys projects separately.
