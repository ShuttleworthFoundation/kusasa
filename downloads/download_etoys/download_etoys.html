Kusasa eToys projects

Our eToys projects are like Lego instructions. The Thunderbolt Kids provide step-by-step instructions for building something in eToys. We expect that some people in the Squeak community may question why we spoonfeed learners with almost every single step in the construction process.

There are two reasons for this:
1) Kusasa is designed to operate as far as possible without an expert teacher.
2) You can follow our projects to build something in eToys, but this doesn't mean you are limited to building only this. Like Lego instructions, once you've built the object you can use what you've learned to build your own eToys creations.

So, while these eToys projects are not constructivism at its best, they contribute to the development of a constructing mindset, which can be put to work on free form projects
