Kusasa Level 1

While intended for grade four learners, the eight modules that make up Kusasa Level 1 are suitable for grade 5 and grade 6 learners who have not yet been exposed to eToys.
Kusasa level 1

Kusasa levels 1, 2 and 3 are packaged together in LAMS modules which can be downloaded here. The stories are contained within the LAMS modules, however all the stories are also accessible online.
